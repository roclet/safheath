Overview of Safhealth Apps

Safhealth apps enhance daily healthcare access,reduce health service provider workload, and minimize client wait times.

Health Services Providers

  • Register online and offer services.
  • Create and manage consultation slots.
  • Post and book healthcare locum jobs.
  • Acknowledge service commission fees.

Healthcare Locum

  • Register as a locum professional.
  • Set availability slots.
  • Find and book locum jobs.

Clients

  • Register and set location.
  • Select consultation type (Online/In-person).
  • Choose a payment method.
  • Search for health services.
  • Book and confirm appointments.

Subscription Steps

Health Service Providers

  • Download and install the app.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Register service details.
  • Select consultation types and pricing.
  • Acknowledge service fees.
  • Approval by Safhealth admin.
  • Go live for client bookings.

Healthcare Locum

  • Download and install the app.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Register service and category.
  • Create or select availability slots.
  • Approval by Safhealth admin.
  • Book healthcare locum jobs.

Clients

  • Register and set location.
  • Download and install the app.
  • Register or log in.
  • Search and book health services.
  • Confirm payment.

Our Vision, Mission & Services

The Safhealth apps promote a healthier life by facilitating faster and safer access to health services.

Vision

  • Easy health service appointment booking for clients.
  • Easy access and booking of healthcare locum services.
  • Seamless connection between health service providers and healthcare locums.
  • Efficient internal service management for clients.
  • Enhanced communication among healthcare providers.

Mission

  • Connecting healthcare providers, locums, and clients online.
  • Ensuring safe, accessible, and affordable Safhealth app services.

Goals

  • Multi-platform access on mobile, Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.
  • Bridging the gap between health service providers and clients.

About Us

The Safhealth project was founded in 2020 and is a joint venture between two companies:

  • Together the Best Life (TBL): A South African company (Reg. No. 2019/626959/07) with a team of healthcare professionals and a public health officer.
  • Biacibenga Solutions: A South African company (Reg. No. 2021/571950/07) with a team of expert software developers.

The Safhealth project provides access to multiple health services through three specialized mobile applications:

  • Health Services App: For managing healthcare providers and services.
  • Healthcare Professional App: Connecting healthcare professionals with service providers.
  • Clients/Patients App: Allowing clients to book and manage appointments.

Our apps are available for download on the Google Play Store and Apple iStore, along with a web-based platform for seamless healthcare service management.
