ii. The requester must provide sufficient detail on the request form to enable the Designated Information Officer of Safhealth to identify the record and the requester. The requester should also indicate which form of access is required and specify a postal address or fax number in the Republic. The requester should also indicate if, in addition to a written reply, any other manner that is to be used to inform the requester and state the necessary particulars to be so informed.

iii. The requester must identify the right that is sought to be exercised or protected and provide an explanation of why the requested record is required for the exercise or protection of that right.

iv. If a request is made on behalf of another person, the requester must submit proof of the capacity in which the requester is making the request, to the satisfaction of the Designated Information Officer of Safhealth in order to release the information.
